About Us

Maku D & C Hire was formed by two siblings (Linda and Irene) who believe in creating employment rather than seeking for employment in a country which was hit hard by the COVID-19 pandemic as well as brain drain. The company was formed in order to fill in gaps inherent with the foregoing. We stand by  what we believe in, that is,  “Motoring at Affordable Kilometres Unceasingly” (MAKU).  Our services are for all and sundry. The industry can only be revived by committed stakeholders such as ourselves.  

MAKU D & C Hire was formed to provide motoring services to locals as well as tourists/foreigners on business or assignments in Zimbabwe. We are flexible in that our motor vehicles can be hired with or without our own drivers. With the former, our clients are assured of being in the safest hands possible.  The company’s Directors do not take chances by employing drivers with shortcomings. The same can be said of our motor vehicles which are roadworthy. 

Our Mission
